Who We Are

10 Years of Textile Excellence


Swastik Enterprises is a leading processor of export-quality textile fabrics, headquartered in the Textile Valley Industrial Area, Sohna, Haryana. We specialize in dyeing and printing with state-of-the-art machinery.

We use premium DYSTAR & CIBACRON dyes and offer special finishes including Water Repellant, Waterproof, Silicon, and Zero-Zero treatments. Our in-house R&D laboratory ensures consistent quality and product development.

Client relationships built on respect, mutual benefit, and long-lasting partnerships are at the core of our business philosophy.
